About EcoSurya Energies

Transforming agriculture through innovative solar-powered solutions for a sustainable tomorrow

Who We Are

EcoSurya Energies is a forward-thinking renewable energy company dedicated to transforming agriculture and rural communities through solar-powered solutions.

Our innovative products — Solar Motor Pumps and MPPT Controllers — are designed to empower farmers with reliable, cost-efficient, and eco-friendly technologies that reduce dependency on conventional energy sources.

By harnessing the power of the sun, we help farmers enhance productivity, conserve resources, and lower operational costs while contributing to a sustainable future.

Our Mission

At EcoSurya Energies, our mission is to provide innovative, reliable, and affordable solar-powered agricultural motors that:

  • Cut costs for farmers
  • Promote eco-friendly farming practices
  • Enhance agricultural productivity
  • Preserve the environment through renewable energy
